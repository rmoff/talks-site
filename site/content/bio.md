Robin is a Principal DevEx Engineer. He has been speaking at conferences since 2009 including QCon, Devoxx, Strata, Kafka Summit, and Øredev. You can find his [talks online](https://talks.rmoff.net), subscribe to his [YouTube channel](http://youtube.com/rmoff) (thanks lockdown!), and read [his blog](http://rmoff.net/). Outside of work he enjoys drinking good beer and eating fried breakfasts, although generally not at the same time.
